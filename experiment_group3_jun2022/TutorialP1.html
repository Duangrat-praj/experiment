This part is intended to familiarize you with the procedures of the experiment.

  • You will be presented with a series of 5 images that contain different patterns.
    Each pattern is associated with different score as shown below.

    • Your job is to mentally calculate and report the total scores from the series of images you are shown. The total payoff can be either positive or negative.

  • After that you will be asked whether you want to see how many pictures of you just saw.

  • If you do not want to see, you can click NO button and the game ends. Your final answer will be the same as your first answer.

  • If you want to see, you can click YES button and ANSWER the total score again. Then, the game ends. The new answer will be your final answer.
